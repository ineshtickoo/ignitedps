Epsilon 2020 Intras Prompt

The following are the prompts for Flux Epsilon Intras 2020. You may provide a solution for either one of the prompts, based on the guidelines here.

  1. Isolation

NASA astronauts are trained to live and work in the isolation of space, and other types of NASA professionals have experience enduring social isolation as well. Oceanographers and cryospheric researchers can spend months on ships or at remote locations conducting research. Terrestrial scientists can also spend weeks or months isolated in the study of local biology, ecosystems, or fieldwork. For some professionals, social distancing is a normal practice, but for countless people, the experience of isolation is a new and perhaps challenging experience.

Your task is to design a system that solves this problem of deep isolation that researchers and astronauts face. This solution can be in the form of an app or a concept, a device, or anything that comes to your mind and can solve this problem. You will be judged on the basis of

  1. Radiation Shielding

“Beyond Low Earth Orbit, space radiation may place astronauts at significant risk for radiation sickness and increased lifetime risk for cancer, central nervous system effects, and degenerative diseases. Research studies of exposure in various doses and strengths of radiation provide strong evidence that cancer and degenerative diseases are to be expected from exposures to galactic cosmic rays (GCR) or solar particle events (SPE).”

  • https://www.nasa.gov/analogs/nsrl/why-space-radiation-matters

Your task is to identify the problem of space radiation and implore on a conceptual solution that helps in shielding astronauts from radiation. A concept that is technically possible (now or in near future), conceptually and economically viable will be expected. The solution can be in the form of modifications or any other bioengineered external devices that may help with the same. These are just a few examples and you may not need to follow the path of either, the sky is the limit with imagination.

Needless to say, for either topic, no plagiarism will be tolerated.

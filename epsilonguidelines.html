Epsilon Guidelines

Two prompts will be released on 25th June 2020, 12:00 PM noon. You can choose to work on any one of them. The prompts will provide you with a brief statement problem to which you have to propose a solution to.

  • You may refer to the internet for research.
  • You have to provide a theoretical model that explains the intricacies of how  and why your proposal is viable. Use of graphs, diagrams or similar visual tools is encouraged albeit not necessary.

Judging criteria:

  1. Feasibility of the solution
  2. Technical Superiority
  3. Innovation
  4. Cost Viability
  5. Prompt Relevance
